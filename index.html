Where Ancient Traditions and
Modern Marvels Unite

Japan's tourism sector is a shining example of a country that skillfully blends the most recent modern technology with its deeply ingrained traditions, making it a popular travel destination for people all over the world. There are many different sights in the Land of the Rising Sun that make an unforgettable impression on tourists.

Shinjuku's glittering skyscrapers and Asakusa's old Senso-ji Temple live in harmony in Tokyo, the dynamic city that never sleeps. But Kyoto, with its centuries-old temples and shrines, is a live example of Japan's rich cultural legacy, taking you back in time.
